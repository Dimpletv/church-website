Organisations in our Church

MGOCSM

Welcome to the Youth League, the MGOCSM of St. John's Indian Orthodox Church Drexel Hill, PA! We are an Orthodox Christian organization determined to enhance the spiritual lives of the Orthodox youth through “worship, study, and service” within the practices and beliefs of the Orthodox Christian faith established in the Three Holy Ecumenical Councils. Our mission is to bring Orthodox Christian students of different educational institutions into one body in order to root them in the Orthodox faith and traditions and to strengthen them in spiritual life and Christian fellowship.

Treasurer: Mr. Nidhin Chacko

OUR PARISH SUNDAY SCHOOL

There is a well organized Sunday school in our Parish level for nurturing our children in the faith of the Holy Trinity, Jesus Christ as the Lord and Savior and in the faith and traditions of Orthodox Church. Our Area Sunday School Inspector is Mr. Mathew Samuel and Mr. Paul J. Maret is serving as the headmaster and Mrs. Mercy Varkey as the assistant headmistress. Our Parish Sunday School takes the form of one hour Bible and prescribed text study before our Church Sunday service. Teachers are mostly well experienced volunteered lay people.

MARTHA MARIAM SAMAJAM

Martha Mariam Samajan [Women’s Organization] is one of the major spiritual organizations of the Malankara Orthodox Church uniting all the female members under cross of our Lord. The organization was founded in 1920 aimed at the spiritual progress of women. Now this organization is spread over all parishes of the twenty-four dioceses of the Church across the world. The Vicar of the parish is the President of the parish level Samajam, who gives guidance and support to the activities of each parish wing

The current leadership positions are as follows:

Secretary: Mrs. Annamma Bensen

Jt. Secretary: Mrs. Reena Daniel

Treasurer: Mrs. Jessy Oommen

FOCUS

The Philadelphia Chapter intends to bring together all the young Orthodox adults living in and around Philadelphia (PA and South Jersey) who are interested in worshipping and serving the Lord in this new era of our Church in America. FOCUS in Philadelphia is set up with its strength, being at the grassroots level. It is inter-parish, with all young adults from all parishes in the Philadelphia area participating. Communication and the sharing of resources is essential for the success of this movement. Gatherings deviate from the traditional bible studies, or conference. This group benefits from the active participation of the priests, deacons in the local parishes.

The current leadership positions are as follows:

President: Rev. Fr. Kuriakose Siby Varghese

Secretary: Mr. Abraham Thomas

Jt. Secretary: Mrs. Lynne George

Treasurer: Mr. Lijish George
